Kids’ Jewellery Designs: Accessorising Childhood

Lions, and tigers, and bears, oh my! Lions, and tigers, and bears, oh my! These lines uttered by the characters in the Hollywood classic The Wizard of Oz seem appropriate to describe the kids’ jewellery designs at BlueStone. However, in this case, they are said with awe at the sheer variety on offer.

You could choose earrings, rings, pendants, and necklaces for your child or a favourite niece and nephew through the kids’ jewellery design catalogue available online. There are snowflakes, bears, lions, tigers, flowers, penguins, dolphins, strawberries, butterflies and all those fantastic critters and objects that make childhood a place of wonder and magic, now available in the form of wearable jewellery.

Buy Kids' Jewellery Designs: Choose BlueStone

At BlueStone, we have a stunning range of kids' jewellery. Take a look at some of the trending pieces.

Buy Kids Jewellery Designs: Buy Pendants

A small blue and black car zooming across the horizon: the Zip Zap Zoom Pendant for Kids made with 18kt gold is exactly what your car-crazy child might appreciate as a birthday gift, as a reward for good behaviour or a special treat for doing well in school. If you need a more feminine touch, you could buy her the Floral Princess Pendant for Kids that is a little white flower with a pink bow, all made in 18kt gold. The Nifty Cootie Pendant for Kids is patterned like a bug with three teeny-weeny diamonds adorning the body made of 18kt gold.

For something gender neutral, we have pendants like the Smiley Baby Pendant for Kids made in 18kt gold, with diamonds on the three-point crown worn by the yellow coloured smiley; the Kiddie Panda Pendant for Kids is again another gender neutral 18kt gold pendant shaped like a cute little panda in white and black.

Earring Designs for Kids

There are a number of earring designs to choose from our collection for your little girl. The Elsa Star Drop Earrings for Kids is inspired by the Disney movie Frozen. It is made in white gold with a star-shaped, blue rimmed stud set with diamonds; and hanging blue topaz stones finish the look of these adorable earrings. Who wouldn’t like the Favourite Dolphin Earrings for Kids shaped like two leaping dolphins? Is there a formal event around the corner? The Classic Pearl Stud for Kids is a classic single pearl set that can be worn by your little girl for the occasion. The tortoise-shaped Tortoise Earrings for Kids set in 22kt gold is another design that is unique and finely crafted.

Your tiny princess would love the Pretty Butterfly Kids Detachable Huggies design, which comes with small detachable pink butterflies. The main 22kt gold ring has white detailing on the front and can be worn as standalone earrings. Or you could buy her the very stylish Reggina Earrings for Kids. These are 18kt gold studs, one in the shape of a star and the other in the shape of the crescent moon. She would find the odd pair with a twinkle of diamond each in the middle irresistible.

Buy Kids' Jewellery Online: Say Yourself from Tantrums

Buying kids’ jewellery had never been easier. Our kids’ jewellery designs are available online. You could include your little tyke in the decision-making without being subject to a public tantrum, bored tears and messy clothes from the comfort of your home. Your little princess or prince would be happy that they get to choose something for themselves, while you do not have to worry about misplacing a valuable piece or something being mishandled by tiny hands.

What’s more, if something takes your fancy, but requires a bit of a tweak, then we offer customisation options. And voila, your child has a unique piece to wear that makes her or him stand out from the crowd. The kids’ jewellery prices at BlueStone are affordable and will not make a dent in your pockets. So buy kids’ jewellery designs from Bluestone and accessorise your tiny fashionista at the earliest.
